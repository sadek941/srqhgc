Hurricane Protection

Sarasota Hurricane Season Is Real. We Take It Seriously.

Florida's Gulf Coast is one of the most hurricane-vulnerable regions in the country. For part-time residents and snowbirds, a storm while you're away can turn into a catastrophe — because no one is there to prepare, respond, or report back.

SRQ HGC provides professional hurricane preparation and response as part of every plan from Coastal and above — and available as an add-on for Essential members.

  • ✦ Storm shutter installation and outdoor furniture securing
  • ✦ Generator inspection and fuel check before storm season
  • ✦ Water and gas shutoff procedures if needed
  • ✦ Immediate post-storm property inspection with photo documentation
  • ✦ Damage assessment and emergency contractor coordination
  • ✦ Real-time updates to you no matter where you are
  • ✦ Seasonal storm prep checklist completed before June 1st every year
