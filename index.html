Hurricane Protection

Florida storms don't wait — your home shouldn't either

Living on Florida's Gulf Coast means hurricane season is a real threat every single year. Sarasota, Longboat Key, Siesta Key, and Casey Key are all directly in the path of storms that form in the Gulf and Atlantic. The difference between a home that weathers a storm and one that suffers catastrophic damage often comes down to preparation — and who you have watching over it.

Pre-Storm Preparation

We deploy shutters, store outdoor furniture, document your home with photos, and secure all entry points before a storm makes landfall — even while you're out of state.

Post-Storm Inspection

Within hours of a storm passing, we inspect your property for damage, photograph everything, and contact you immediately with a full report so you can make informed decisions fast.

Emergency Vendor Coordination

If damage is found, we coordinate emergency contractors, board-up crews, and remediation companies on your behalf — no waiting, no out-of-town panic calls required.

Insurance Documentation

Our timestamped photo reports provide the documentation insurance companies need. We keep a full maintenance and condition history so your claim is backed by real evidence.

🌟 Hurricane prep and post-storm inspection is included in our Coastal, SRQ Care, Estate Care, and Sarasota Life plans. Essential members can add it as an upgrade. Don't wait until a storm is in the Gulf — by then it's too late.

Welcome Home Package

What to expect — the details that matter

Transparency is everything. Here is exactly how our services work, what's included, and what you should know before signing up.

Service Details & Fine Print

Welcome Home Package — How It Works

The Welcome Home Package is a one-time-per-visit preparation service activated when you notify us you are arriving. It includes a full property walkthrough, fresh linens turned down, lights and AC set to your preference, pantry and fridge stocked with basics (if requested), welcome items placed, and a full inspection completed before you walk in the door. This service is included once per month on our Coastal, SRQ Care, Estate Care, and Sarasota Life plans. Additional Welcome Home preparations beyond the one included per month are available as an add-on service at $75 per visit.

Smart Home Integration

SRQ HGC coordinates with and monitors your existing smart home systems as part of all membership tiers. This includes checking smart locks, thermostat settings, leak sensors, security cameras (view-only access), and smart lighting. We do not install new systems, but we work with whatever you have in place. For members interested in upgrading their smart home setup, we can coordinate installation through our trusted vendor network.

Visit Frequency by Plan

Essential members receive bi-weekly (every two weeks) inspections. Coastal and SRQ Care members receive weekly visits. Estate Care and Sarasota Life members receive twice-weekly visits. Additional visits can be added to any plan during active hurricane season or at any time for an additional fee. Visit schedules are fixed by day of week during your onboarding and adjusted with 48-hour notice.

Vendor Coordination

SRQ HGC does not directly provide pool cleaning, lawn care, HVAC service, or other trade services. We coordinate and oversee your existing vendors or help you find trusted local providers. We are present for vendor visits when needed and confirm work is completed correctly. Essential plan includes observation and reporting only. Coastal includes 1 active vendor coordination per month. SRQ Care, Estate Care, and Sarasota Life include unlimited vendor coordination.

Emergency Response

All plans include emergency notification — we will contact you immediately if we discover a problem at your property. Active emergency coordination (contacting vendors, supervising repairs) is included in SRQ Care, Estate Care, and Sarasota Life. 24/7 response is available on Estate Care and Sarasota Life. Essential and Coastal members can reach us during business hours (8am–6pm, 7 days a week).

Annual Plan Billing

Annual plans are billed upfront in full and represent a 15% discount versus paying month-to-month. Annual plans are non-refundable after the 30-day cancellation window. Month-to-month plans require 30 days written notice to cancel. There are no setup fees, no contracts beyond the monthly/annual agreement, and no hidden charges. Any add-on services are billed monthly in arrears.

Service Area

SRQ HGC currently serves properties in Sarasota County, including Longboat Key, Siesta Key, Casey Key, Palmer Ranch, Lakewood Ranch, Downtown Sarasota, Bird Key, St. Armands Key, and surrounding areas. Properties outside our standard service area may be subject to a travel fee. Contact us to confirm coverage for your specific address.
