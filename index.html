Hurricane Protection

Sarasota Hurricane Season Is Real. We Take It Seriously.

Florida's Gulf Coast is one of the most hurricane-vulnerable regions in the country. For part-time residents, a storm while you're away can turn into a catastrophe — because no one is there to prepare, respond, or report back.

SRQ HGC provides professional hurricane preparation and response as part of every plan from Coastal and above — and available as a low-cost add-on for Essential members.

  • Storm shutter installation and outdoor furniture securing
  • Generator inspection and fuel check before storm season
  • Water and gas shutoff procedures when needed
  • Immediate post-storm inspection with photo documentation
  • Emergency contractor coordination for damage response
  • Real-time updates to you no matter where you are
  • Seasonal storm prep checklist completed each June
