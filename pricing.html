Welcome Home Package

Available as an add-on to any plan — here's everything you should know

What's Included

Fresh flowers, stocked pantry essentials, fresh linens turned down, AC set to your preference, lights on, and a personal welcome note — so your home is warm and ready the moment you arrive.

How It Works

Schedule at least 48 hours before arrival. We'll coordinate everything remotely so there's no need for you to manage a single detail. Available for all membership tiers as an add-on.

Pricing

$99 per preparation for members (regularly $175 standalone). No minimum frequency required — use it when you need it.

What's Not Included

Cost of groceries, flowers, or consumables are billed separately at actual cost. We do not mark up supplies — you pay exactly what we pay.

Fine Print

Welcome Home Package add-on is available to active members only. Scheduling requires a minimum 48-hour advance notice. Last-minute requests (under 48 hours) may be accommodated at a $40 rush fee. Supply costs (groceries, flowers, etc.) are billed at actual cost with zero markup. The Welcome Home Package does not include cleaning services — if a cleaning is needed prior to arrival, this can be arranged through vendor coordination. Welcome Home Package usage is not limited — you may schedule it as often as you visit.
