Welcome Home Package — Full Details

Available as an Add-On to Any Plan

What's Included

Fresh flowers, stocked pantry essentials, linens turned down, AC set to your preference, lights on, and a personal welcome note — so your home is warm and ready the moment you walk in.

How It Works

Schedule at least 48 hours before arrival. We coordinate everything remotely — no need for you to manage a single detail. Available for all membership tiers as an add-on.

Pricing

$99/preparation for members (reg. $175 standalone). No minimum frequency — use it whenever you visit.

What's Not Included

Cost of groceries, flowers, or consumables billed separately at actual cost. Zero markup — you pay exactly what we pay.

Fine Print

Available to active members only. Minimum 48-hour advance notice required. Rush requests under 48 hours may be accommodated with a $40 surcharge. Supply costs (groceries, flowers, etc.) billed at actual cost with zero markup. Does not include cleaning services — cleaning can be coordinated separately through vendor coordination. No limit on frequency — use as often as you visit.
