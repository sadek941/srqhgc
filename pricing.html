Welcome Home Package — Full Details

Available as an Add-On to Any Plan

What's Included

Fresh flowers, stocked pantry essentials, linens turned down, AC set to your preferred temperature, lights on, and a personal welcome note from our team.

How It Works

Schedule at least 48 hours before your arrival. We coordinate everything remotely — no need for you to manage a single detail. Just walk in and feel at home.

Pricing

$99 per preparation for members (regular standalone rate: $175). No minimum frequency — use it whenever you visit. Supply costs billed at actual cost, zero markup.

What's Not Included

Cost of groceries, flowers, or consumables billed at actual cost. Zero markup — you pay exactly what we pay. Cleaning services coordinated separately if needed.

Fine Print

Available to active members only. 48-hour advance notice required. Rush requests under 48 hours may be accommodated with a $40 surcharge. Supply costs billed at actual cost, zero markup. Does not include cleaning — cleaning can be coordinated through vendor coordination. No limit on frequency.
