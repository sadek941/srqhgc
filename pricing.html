🏠 Welcome Home Package — What's Included

Included once per month on Coastal, SRQ Care, Estate Care, and Sarasota Life plans. Activated when you notify us you are arriving. We complete a full property walkthrough, set your AC and lights to preference, turn down fresh linens, place welcome items, and send a confirmation photo report before you walk through the door. Additional Welcome Home preparations beyond the one included per month are available as an add-on at $75 per visit.

📝 Plan Details & Fine Print

Annual Billing

Annual plans are billed upfront and receive a 10% discount. There is a 30-day cancellation window from the start date. After 30 days, annual plans are non-refundable.

Smart Home Integration

Included in all plans. We monitor your existing smart locks, thermostats, leak sensors, and security cameras (view-only). We do not install systems but coordinate installation through our vendor network.

Cancellation Policy

Month-to-month plans require 30 days written notice to cancel. No setup fees, no hidden charges. Add-on services are billed monthly in arrears.

Vendor Coordination

SRQ HGC coordinates and oversees vendors but does not directly provide trade services. Essential: observation only. Coastal: 1 coordination/month. SRQ Care and above: unlimited coordination.
