🏠 Welcome Home Package — Included on Coastal, SRQ Care, Estate Care & Sarasota Life

The Welcome Home Package is activated when you notify us you are arriving. We complete a full walkthrough, set your AC and thermostat to preference, turn on lights, turn down fresh linens, place welcome items, and send you a confirmation photo report before you step through the door. Included once per month on qualifying plans. Additional Welcome Home preparations available at $75 per visit.

📝 Plan Details & Fine Print

Annual Billing & 15% Savings

Annual plans are billed upfront in full at a 15% discount versus month-to-month pricing. There is a 30-day cancellation window from start date. After 30 days, annual plans are non-refundable. Month-to-month plans require 30 days written notice to cancel. No setup fees, no hidden charges.

Smart Home Integration

Included in all plans. We monitor your existing smart locks, thermostats, leak sensors, and security cameras (view-only). We do not install systems but coordinate installation through our trusted vendor network at your request.

Vendor Coordination

SRQ HGC coordinates and oversees vendors but does not directly provide trade services. Essential: observation & reporting only. Coastal: 1 active coordination per month. SRQ Care and above: unlimited vendor coordination and supervision.

Emergency Response

All plans include emergency notification. Active emergency coordination is included in SRQ Care and above. 24/7 response with a dedicated direct line is available on Estate Care and Sarasota Life. Essential and Coastal reach us during extended business hours (8am–8pm, 7 days/week).

Visit Scheduling

Visit schedules are set by day of week during onboarding. Changes require 48-hour notice. Additional visits beyond your plan are available at any time for an add-on fee — recommended during active hurricane season.

Service Area

We serve all of Sarasota County including Longboat Key, Siesta Key, Casey Key, Bird Key, St. Armands Key, Palmer Ranch, Lakewood Ranch, and Downtown Sarasota. Properties outside our standard area may carry a travel supplement — contact us to confirm.
